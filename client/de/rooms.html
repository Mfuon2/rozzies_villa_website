Rozzie's Villa Rooms

About our Rooms

Rozzie Beach Villa comfortably sleeps up to 8 persons. It has four fully equipped rooms with a high ceiling that gives it a wide and airy feel.

Our rooms feature internet connectivity as well as air conditioning. Thanks to our skilled crew, we can fulfill any cleaning, laundry, and hospitality demands on a daily basis.


On the top floor, the master bedroom is approximately 35 square meters.

It has a King Size bed, bed linen, a mosquito net, cupboards, air conditioners, a dressing mirror, and an en-suite bathroom with hot shower, towels, and toilet.

It also has a large balcony with a lovely couch that gives a great view of the Indian Ocean and the swimming pool.


Three King-sized bedrooms all with air conditioning, internet connectivity, and contemporary walk-in showers are located on the ground level.
