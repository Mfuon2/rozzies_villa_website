About Rozzie's Villa

If you've decided to take your turn experiencing the wonders of this very unique region of the world, choosing Rozzies Beach Villa ensures that you'll get the most out of your vacation/wedding celebrations. We don't merely believe in the richness of the lacquer or the attention to detail, but also in something more intangible: the soul of a place that must also provide our guests with an amazing beach vacation. This is the perfect escape for honeymooners, friends, families, and luxury travelers wishing to unwind and rejuvenate while absorbing the African sun.

Rozzies Beach Villa is an absolute privacy ideal spot that delivers an Oasis of leisure, nestled within the Kenyan shore. Our rooms are large and comfortable, with nice views of the ocean and the lush tropical gardens that line their banks. All guests are treated to a sense of well-being while being pampered with the best services and hospitality. We ensure that our guests have the opportunity to sample a variety of Swahili delicacies, continental cuisines, fresh fish, and locally grown products, all of which are delivered on a daily basis.

The Colorful Villa offers limitless internet access, secure parking, and 24-hour security. To guarantee the safety of our guests, we also adopt the COVID 19 protocols.
