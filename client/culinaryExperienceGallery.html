Kitchen

The Villa offers a completely equipped modern kitchen capable of meeting even the most demanding needs that will tempt you to prepare delectable meals. On request, we provide first-class chef service as well as exhibit traditional local recipes.



Dinning

Our dining rooms are tastefully furnished with exquisitely crafted hardwood tables and chairs. This beautiful, homely dining area is decorated with soul-soothing artworks and room decor. Here you can comfortably enjoy your fusion of African, European ,Italian or Indian cuisine.

Sitting Room

The sitting room faces the seaside and features distinctive furniture carved in the Swahili style, beautiful natural hues, and good pillows that provide comfort. It's also surrounded by vibrant palm trees and tropical plants/flowers, so you can inhale the fragrances while sipping your beverage.

Rozzies Villa

Rozzies villa is indulgently spacious luxurious villa offering sweeping views of the ocean and the lush tropical gardens spilling over their banks. A gorgeous place to enjoy life
