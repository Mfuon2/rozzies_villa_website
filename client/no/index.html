About Us

If you've decided to take your turn experiencing the wonders of this very unique region of the world, picking Rozzies Beach Villa ensures that you'll get the most out of your vacation/wedding celebrations. We don't merely believe in the richness of the lacquer or the attention to detail, but also in something more intangible: the soul of a place that must also provide our guests with an amazing beach vacation. This is the perfect escape for honeymooners, friends, families, and luxury travelers wishing to unwind and rejuvenate while absorbing the African sun.

Rozzies Beach Villa is an absolute privacy ideal spot that delivers an Oasis of leisure, nestled within the Kenyan shore. Our rooms are luxuriously large and comfortable, with panoramic views of the ocean or the lush tropical gardens that line their banks. All guests are treated to a sense of well-being while being pampered with the best services and hospitality. We ensure that our guests have the opportunity to sample a variety of Swahili delicacies, continental cuisines, fresh fish, and locally grown products, all of which are delivered on a daily basis.

The Colorful Villa offers limitless internet access, secure parking, and 24-hour security. To guarantee the safety of our guests, we also adopt the COVID 19 protocols.

Local Attractions

Rozzies Beach Villa contains a range of arranged experiences, including pick-ups near historical homes, museums, monuments, hikes, amusement parks, waterparks, game viewing around towns, and much more.

Kilifi's inhabitants peacefully coexist with our guests, reinforcing our value as more than just a multicultural background and civilized people. Let’s not even talk about the local food! It is amazingly delicious! The art and crafts from the nearby curio shops make you feel the warmth of Africa.

Activities

Rozzies Beach Villa is more than just a place to unwind. Both indoor and outdoor activities are available. We feature an in-house recreation area with a variety of games such as table tennis, a pool table, and darts. We provide water activities for outdoor sports enthusiasts, such as windsurfing, kitesurfing, snorkeling, among scuba diving, where skilled instructors will assist you to pick your equipment on rent and take you step by step through this fantastic feeling.
