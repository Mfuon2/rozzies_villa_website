Frequently Asked Questions

In order for you to get answer to your question as quick as possible we have gathered the most frequently asked questions here.

  • Is the villa standard?

    The Villa is VIP in all levels.

  • How big is the Villa?

    The Villa has a big hall, a dining room with a cocktail bar and a huge kitchen.

  • How many rooms does the Villa have?

    The Villa has four big suites all fitted with king sized beds and internet connection.

  • What indoor activities are available at the Villa

    A beautiful room open up to the makuti roof with a nine ft pool table, table tennis, dart and a smart TV.

  • Availability of internet?

    Good signal both inside and outside the villa.

  • Does the villa have a sunbed?

    The villa has romantic double sunbeds under a pergola.

  • Does the Villa have Outside dining?

    The villa has a nice gazebo by the pool with a pizza oven. There is also a huge terrace by the house and a terrace by the sea viewpoint.

  • Does the villa have a beach line?

    The villa has a 200ft beach line right into the Indian Ocean.

  • Does the villa have a garden?

    The villa has an exotic green palm garden with a lot of nice spots.

  • Does the villa have a parking?

    The villa has a huge garage and plenty of parking space in front of the villa.

  • What services are available at the villa?

    The villa has a full staffed with a manager, housekeeper, gardener and a cook.

  • How is the security at the villa?

    The villa has a night guard with a direct line to the KK security base.

  • What kind of entertainment is available?

    The entertainment will be provided upon request.
